vectorstores is a **TypeScript framework for building AI applications on top of your data**.
It's an abstract layer on top of vector stores, providing a unified interface for working with them.
It gives you composable building blocks for **ingestion** and **retrieval**, so you can ship production-grade AI applications getting their context from your data.

Built for modern JavaScript runtimes like **Node.js**, **Deno**, **Bun**, **Cloudflare Workers**, and more.

## What is ‚Äúcontext engineering‚Äù?

LLMs are powerful, but they don‚Äôt know your private or domain data by default.
**Context engineering** is the practice of selecting the right slices of your data and injecting them into the prompt at the right time.

The most common pattern is **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**:

- **Indexing stage**: ingest raw inputs, chunk them into `Node`s, and compute embeddings.
- **Querying stage**: retrieve the most relevant `Node`s for a query, optionally rerank them, then generate an answer grounded in that context.

Learn the mental model in the [RAG concepts guide](/tutorials/rag/concepts).

## Key building blocks

- **Documents & Nodes**: your data as `Document`s, split into retrievable `Node`s. See [Documents and Nodes](/modules/data).
- **Ingestion pipelines**: configurable transformations (splitters, embeddings). See [Ingestion Pipeline](/modules/data/ingestion_pipeline).
- **Retrievers**: fetch relevant `Node`s for a query. See [Retriever](/modules/rag/retriever).
- **Postprocessors**: rerank, filter, or otherwise transform retrieved nodes. Example: [Cohere Reranker](/modules/rag/node_postprocessors/cohere_reranker).
